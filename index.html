About Us

Harakati logistics is a wholly zimbabwean owned well-established road freight transport and courier company, located in Msasa, Harare, Zimbabwe. The business transports a variety of general cargo and goods in southern Africa. All our drivers, Crane operators and general workers are fully trained and all employees and trucks comply with the safety standards.

I

Safety

Safety & Quality Assessment for Sustainability through the Chemical & Allied Industries Association in South Africa at set intervals. Strict Health, Safety, Security & Environmental Policies are in place and adhered to Driver Safety Standards

I

Tracking

We offer 24-hour satellite tracking (C-Track) to keep our clients up to date and we can constantly locate client products to within 2 meters and 5 minutes of their product location. In the event of challenges with traditional telecommunication networks, our system automatically switches to satellite communications and keeps us in constant contact with our drivers.

I

Training

Drivers undergo full training before employment and are re-trained annually through our in-house program. Every driver is fully compliant and conversant with the legal requirements laid out in theOccupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993,Transportation of Dangerous Goods Regulations as set outin the National Road Traffc Act 93 of 1996 and the National Environmental Management Act of Zimbabwe.
