Who We Are

We are a wholly Zimbabwean owned well-established road freight transport and courier company. We transports a variety of general cargo and goods in southern Africa. All our drivers, Crane operators and general workers are fully trained and all employees and trucks comply with the safety standards.

R
Mission

Its goal is to provide the highest level of transportation services as well as safe and timely deliveries, with fair and competitive pricing while providing a safe workplace for employees and maintaining integrity, fairness and honesty with customers and business partners.

R
Vision

Harakati Logistic's mission is to become the recognized leader in Zimbabwe's transport industry for long and short distance trucking and transportation of goods.
